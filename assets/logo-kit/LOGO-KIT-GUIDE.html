ContextWhich AssetMin SizeNotes

Social media profileIcon mark on white170×170Circular crop safe — logo centered with padding
Social media cover/headerFull logo with tagline820×312Use platform-specific sizes from the kit

Print materialsLetterhead 1200w300 DPI1200w = ~4" at 300 DPI, suitable for A4/Letter

Dark UI / Dark modeDark BG variants—Logo reads well on #1A202C and similar dark surfaces
Photo watermarkWatermark (25% opacity)—Overlay at ~20% of image width for subtlety
Clear space: Always maintain at least 10% of the logo width as clear space around all sides. Never stretch, rotate, or alter the colors of the logo.
