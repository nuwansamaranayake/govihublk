About GoviHub

GoviHub is Sri Lanka's AI-powered farming marketplace — rebuilt around the farmer, not the middleman.

Our mission

Put agricultural technology directly in the hands of Sri Lanka's farmers. Build tools in Sinhala first, not as an afterthought. Make AI useful in a paddy field, a spice garden, a home orchard.

We believe youth will return to farming when farming returns a fair income.

Who builds GoviHub

GoviHub is built by AiGNITE Consulting, a Houston-based technology firm with a Sri Lanka branch. The core platform is engineered in the US; the on-the-ground partnerships, farmer onboarding, and Sinhala content are run from Sri Lanka.

The team behind this launch

  • Nuwan Samaranayake — Founder, AiGNITE Consulting. Product and platform lead.
  • Aruni Apsara — Sri Lanka operations. Sinhala content, farmer communication, outreach.
  • Gamini Pemasiri — Field partnerships. Farmer onboarding, cooperative relationships, pilot coordination.

Partnerships

GoviHub is building in partnership with organizations already working with Sri Lankan farmers:

  • DevPro Sri Lanka — training and agricultural extension services.
  • OXFAM Sri Lanka — smallholder support pathways in the dry zone.

We're open to collaborations with cooperatives, agricultural NGOs, and public-sector agricultural initiatives. Contact us if you're working in this space.

What's built, what's coming

The spices sector is live at spices.govihublk.com. Fruits and produce sectors are planned — the technology, content, and partnerships are already being assembled.
