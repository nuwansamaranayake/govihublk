## 1. Getting Started

GoviHub Spices connects Sri Lankan spice farmers directly to verified commercial buyers. This guide walks you through every feature available to you as a farmer.

### 1.1 What You Need

- A smartphone with internet access (Android or iPhone)
- Chrome, Safari, or any modern web browser
- Photos of your crops (for disease diagnosis)

> **✅ Install as App:** Visit spices.govihublk.com in Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install GoviHub as an app on your phone. It works offline for basic features.

### 1.2 Creating Your Account

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Open GoviHub | Go to spices.govihublk.com in your browser |
| 2 | Tap Register | On the landing page, tap the "Register" button |
| 3 | Enter Your Details | Username, password, full name, phone number, and district |
| 4 | Select Your Role | Choose "Farmer" as your role |
| 5 | Select Your Crops | Pick the spices you grow: pepper, turmeric, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, cardamom, or cinnamon |
| 6 | Set Your Location | Allow GPS access or enter your district manually. This is used for weather forecasts and buyer matching |
| 7 | Done! | You will be taken to your Farmer Dashboard |

> **⚠️ Remember Your Credentials:** Write down your username and password. If you forget them, an admin will need to reset your account.

## 2. Your Dashboard

The dashboard is your home screen. It shows everything important at a glance:

- Weather summary for your location (today's temperature, rain forecast)
- Your active harvest listings and their status
- Recent matches with buyers
- Notification badge showing unread alerts

> **✅ Check Daily:** Open GoviHub every morning to see weather alerts and new buyer matches. The weather card on your dashboard shows the first 3 days of reliable forecasts.

## 3. Creating Harvest Listings

A harvest listing tells buyers what you have available to sell. This is the core of how you connect with buyers on GoviHub.

### 3.1 How to Create a Listing

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Go to Listings | Tap "Listings" from the bottom navigation bar or the More menu |
| 2 | Tap "Create New" | The create listing form will open |
| 3 | Select Crop Type | Choose from: black pepper, turmeric, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, cardamom, cinnamon, or mixed spices |
| 4 | Enter Quantity | How many kilograms you have available |
| 5 | Set Your Price | Price per kilogram in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR) |
| 6 | Add Quality Grade | Select the grade of your harvest (Grade A, B, or C) |
| 7 | Set Available Date | When the harvest will be ready for collection |
| 8 | Upload Photo | Take a photo of your harvest. Buyers prefer listings with photos |
| 9 | Add Description | Any extra details: organic, dried/fresh, processing method, etc. |
| 10 | Submit | Your listing goes live and the matching engine starts looking for buyers |

### 3.2 Managing Your Listings

After creating a listing, you can:

- **Edit** — Update quantity, price, or description at any time
- **Change Status** — Mark as sold, expired, or reactivate
- **Delete** — Remove a listing you no longer need

> **✅ Listing Tips:** Keep prices competitive by checking the Price Trends section. Listings with photos get 3x more buyer interest. Update quantity as you sell to avoid over-committing.

## 4. Smart Matching with Buyers

GoviHub's matching engine automatically pairs your harvest listings with buyer demand postings. It scores each potential match based on:

- **Distance** between you and the buyer (35% weight) — closer buyers are preferred
- **Quantity fit** (25%) — does your harvest size match what they need?
- **Date overlap** (25%) — is your harvest ready when they need it?
- **Listing freshness** (15%) — newer listings get a boost

### 4.1 Working with Matches

When a match is found, you'll see it on your Matches page and receive a notification. For each match, you can:

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Review the Match | See the buyer's name, location, requested quantity, and price range |
| 2 | Accept | You agree to supply this buyer. The match moves to "accepted" status |
| 3 | Reject | Not interested. The match is removed |
| 4 | Dismiss | Ignore for now without rejecting |
| 5 | Complete/Fulfill | After delivering the goods, mark the match as completed |

> **⚠️ Reliability Matters:** Accepting a match and then not delivering hurts your profile. Only accept matches you can fulfill. Your fulfillment history builds your reputation.

## 5. AI Crop Disease Diagnosis

If your spice crop looks unhealthy, GoviHub's AI can help identify the problem and suggest treatments.

### 5.1 How to Use Diagnosis

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Go to Diagnosis | Tap "Diagnosis" from the navigation menu |
| 2 | Upload a Photo | Take a clear, close-up photo of the affected leaf or plant part. Good lighting is essential |
| 3 | Wait for Analysis | The AI (Claude Sonnet 4) analyzes your image. This takes 5–15 seconds |
| 4 | Read the Results | You'll see: the identified disease name (in English and Sinhala), treatment recommendations, and prevention advice |
| 5 | Rate the Result | Give feedback on whether the diagnosis was helpful — this improves the system |

> **✅ Better Photos = Better Diagnosis:** Photograph individual leaves showing symptoms. Avoid blurry images. Include both healthy and affected areas for comparison. Natural daylight gives best results.

### 5.2 Diagnosis History

All your past diagnoses are saved. Go to Diagnosis > History to review previous results and track how treatments are working.

## 6. AI Farm Advisory

The Farm Advisory is your personal agricultural consultant. Ask any farming question in Sinhala or English and get expert answers powered by Sri Lankan Department of Agriculture knowledge.

### 6.1 How It Works

The advisory uses a knowledge base of 595 articles covering all 8 spice crops, sourced from Sri Lankan agricultural publications. When you ask a question:

1. Your question is matched against the knowledge base using AI
2. The most relevant articles are retrieved
3. Claude AI generates a detailed answer grounded in those articles, in Sinhala

### 6.2 Example Questions

- "ග්‍රීෂ්ම කාලයට ගම්මිරිස් වෙල හානි වෙනවා?" (What pests harm black pepper in summer?)
- "How do I dry turmeric for best color retention?"
- "ඉඟුරු වෙලට හාරි පහසු කිරීමට මෙන්?" (What is good fertilizer for ginger?)

> **✅ Ask in Sinhala:** The advisory gives the best answers in Sinhala because the knowledge base includes Sinhala agricultural content. You can ask in English too.

## 7. Weather Forecasts & Alerts

GoviHub provides 5-day weather forecasts tailored to your farm location, with specific advice for your spice crops.

### 7.1 Weather Dashboard Card

Your dashboard shows today's weather summary: temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rain probability. The first 3 days are marked as "reliable" and the remaining days as "outlook."

### 7.2 Detailed Forecast

Tap the weather card or go to Weather from the menu to see:

- 5-day forecast with daily high/low temperatures
- Hourly detail for any day (tap a day to expand)
- Soil temperature and moisture data (critical for turmeric, ginger)
- Humidity and wind speed charts

### 7.3 Weather Alerts

GoviHub monitors weather conditions against your crop profiles and sends automatic alerts when:

- Heavy rain threatens drainage (especially for pepper, ginger)
- Temperature drops below critical thresholds for your crops
- High winds could damage vine crops like pepper
- Soil moisture conditions require attention

Each alert comes in Sinhala with a specific action you should take (e.g., "Clear drainage channels").

## 9. Notifications

GoviHub sends you notifications for:

- New buyer matches for your harvest listings
- Match status updates (accepted, completed, etc.)
- Weather alerts for your crops
- Platform announcements from GoviHub admins

The notification bell on your dashboard shows the count of unread notifications. Tap it to see all notifications, and mark them as read individually or all at once.

## 10. Settings & Profile

### 10.1 Profile Settings

Update your personal information: full name, phone number, district and location, land size, and farming experience.

### 10.2 Crop Selection

Go to Settings > My Crops to add or remove spice crops from your profile. This affects which weather alerts you receive and which buyers are matched to you.

### 10.3 Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive. You can enable or disable alerts for matches, weather, prices, and announcements.

### 10.4 Change Password

Go to Settings > Change Password. You'll need your current password to set a new one.
