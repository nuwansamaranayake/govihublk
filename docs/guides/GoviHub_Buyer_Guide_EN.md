## 1. Getting Started

GoviHub Spices connects you directly to Sri Lankan spice farmers — no middlemen, no agents, no wasted trips. Post what you need, and GoviHub's AI matching engine finds the right farmers for you.

### 1.1 What You Need

- A smartphone or computer with internet access
- Chrome, Safari, or any modern web browser

> **✅ Install as App:** Visit spices.govihublk.com in Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install GoviHub as an app on your phone.

### 1.2 Creating Your Account

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Open GoviHub | Go to spices.govihublk.com in your browser |
| 2 | Tap Register | On the landing page, tap the "Register" button |
| 3 | Enter Your Details | Username, password, full name, phone number, and district |
| 4 | Select Your Role | Choose "Buyer" as your role |
| 5 | Complete Your Profile | Enter your business name, buying capacity, and preferred crops |
| 6 | Set Your Location | Allow GPS access or enter your district. This is used to match you with nearby farmers |
| 7 | Done! | You will be taken to your Buyer Dashboard |

> **⚠️ Remember Your Credentials:** Write down your username and password. If you forget them, an admin will need to reset your account.

## 3. Creating Demand Postings

A demand posting tells GoviHub what spices you want to buy. This is how you signal your requirements to the farmer network.

### 3.1 How to Create a Demand Posting

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Go to Demands | Tap "Demands" from the navigation |
| 2 | Tap "Create New" | The create demand form will open |
| 3 | Select Crop Type | Choose the spice you need: black pepper, turmeric, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, cardamom, cinnamon, or mixed spices |
| 4 | Enter Quantity Needed | How many kilograms you want to purchase |
| 5 | Set Your Price Range | Minimum and maximum price per kilogram (LKR) you are willing to pay |
| 6 | Set Quality Grade | Select the minimum grade you accept (Grade A, B, or C) |
| 7 | Set Delivery Window | When you need the supply — start date and end date |
| 8 | Add Requirements | Any special requirements: organic certification, specific drying method, packaging, etc. |
| 9 | Submit | Your demand goes live and the matching engine starts searching for farmers |

### 3.2 Managing Your Demands

After creating a demand posting, you can:

- **Edit** — Update quantity, price range, or delivery window at any time
- **Change Status** — Mark as fulfilled, expired, or reactivate
- **Delete** — Remove a demand you no longer need

> **✅ Demand Tips:** Be specific about quality grade and delivery window — the matching engine uses these to find the best farmer matches. A wider price range gives you more match options.

## 4. Smart Matching with Farmers

GoviHub's AI matching engine automatically pairs your demand postings with farmer harvest listings. It scores each potential match based on:

- **Distance** between you and the farmer (35% weight) — closer farmers reduce transport costs
- **Quantity fit** (25%) — does the farmer's harvest size match your needs?
- **Date overlap** (25%) — is the harvest ready within your delivery window?
- **Listing freshness** (15%) — newer, more current listings get a boost

### 4.1 Working with Matches

When a match is found, you'll see it on your Matches page and receive a notification. For each match, you can:

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| Review | See the farmer's name, location, available quantity, asking price, quality grade, and listing photo |
| Accept | You agree to purchase from this farmer. The farmer is notified |
| Reject | Not interested. The match is removed |
| Dismiss | Ignore for now without rejecting |
| Complete/Fulfill | After receiving the goods, mark the match as completed |

> **✅ Build Your Network:** Over time, your match history helps you identify the most reliable farmers. Completed matches build trust and improve future matching.

## 7. Notifications

GoviHub sends you notifications for:

The notification bell on your dashboard shows the count of unread notifications. Tap it to see all notifications, and mark them as read individually or all at once.
