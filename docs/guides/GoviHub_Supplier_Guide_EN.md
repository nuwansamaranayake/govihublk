## 1. Getting Started

GoviHub Spices gives you direct access to thousands of spice farmers looking for quality farming inputs. List your seeds, fertilizers, tools, and equipment — farmers browse and inquire directly through the app.

### 1.1 What You Need

- A smartphone or computer with internet access
- Chrome, Safari, or any modern web browser
- Photos of your products (listings with photos get more inquiries)

> **✅ Install as App:** Visit spices.govihublk.com in Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen" to use GoviHub like a native app.

### 1.2 Creating Your Account

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Open GoviHub | Go to spices.govihublk.com in your browser |
| 2 | Tap Register | On the landing page, tap the "Register" button |
| 3 | Enter Your Details | Username, password, full name, phone number, and district |
| 4 | Select Your Role | Choose "Supplier" as your role |
| 5 | Complete Your Profile | Enter your business name, product categories, and service area |
| 6 | Set Your Location | Allow GPS access or enter your district |
| 7 | Done! | You will be taken to your Supplier Dashboard |

> **⚠️ Remember Your Credentials:** Write down your username and password. If you forget them, an admin will need to reset your account.

## 3. Creating Supply Listings

A supply listing showcases a product you sell to farmers. Good listings with clear descriptions and photos get the most inquiries.

### 3.1 How to Create a Listing

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Go to Listings | Tap "Listings" from the navigation |
| 2 | Tap "Create New" | The create listing form will open |
| 3 | Select Category | Choose: Seeds, Fertilizer, Pesticide, Tools & Equipment, or Other |
| 4 | Enter Product Name | Clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Organic NPK Fertilizer 50kg") |
| 5 | Set Price | Price in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). Specify if per unit, per kg, or per bag |
| 6 | Enter Description | Detailed product information: composition, usage instructions, brand, pack size |
| 7 | Set Availability | Whether the product is in stock, delivery area, minimum order quantity |
| 8 | Upload Photos | Clear product photos. Multiple angles help farmers make decisions |
| 9 | Submit | Your listing goes live in the supply marketplace |

### 3.2 Managing Your Listings

After creating a listing, you can:

- **Edit** — Update price, description, availability, or photos at any time
- **Delete** — Remove products you no longer sell

> **✅ Listing Tips:** Keep your inventory current — remove or mark products as out of stock when unavailable. Farmers lose trust in suppliers with outdated listings. Include pack sizes and prices clearly.

---

## 4. Handling Farmer Inquiries

When a farmer is interested in your product, they send an inquiry through the app.

| Step | Action | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Go to Inquiries | Tap "Inquiries" from the navigation |
| 2 | View Inquiry | See the farmer's name, location, the product they're asking about, and their message |
| 3 | Respond | Reply with availability, pricing, delivery options, or any additional details |

> **✅ Response Time Matters:** Farmers often need supplies urgently. Quick responses build your reputation and increase repeat business.

## 5. Notifications

GoviHub sends you notifications for:

- New farmer inquiries about your products
- Platform announcements from GoviHub admins

The notification bell on your dashboard shows unread notifications. Tap to view all, and mark as read individually or all at once.
